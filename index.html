About

WELCOME!!! HEAVENLY DELICIOUS SLICE!

A Slice of Heaven box might be one of the most recognisable icons in Kenya.
Whichever way you slice it, Slice of Heaven is an industry leader in the quick service pizza game.
The first Slice of Heaven outlet opened its doors in 2007 in Nairobi, and rose quickly in popularity.
Serving hot oven-fresh pizza with its signature all-natural tomato sauce and using only the freshest ingredients, Slice of Heaven has established itself at the top of the industry and is easily one of the first choices in quick service pizza in Kenya.
